GREEN WORLD

Green World is an online multiplayer game, to help children and grown ups learn the importance of recycling. It was an interactive virtual environment where children can for teams and go through challenges while recycling rubbish correctly. The players couls also take individual and team quests like:

  • building different objects in the virtual world, such as wind mills and solar installations based to game points
  • complete educational quizes
  • make friends with other players they meet in the online world though a messaging system

I was responsible for the whole backend infrastructure for a multiplayer environment, built on top of the Microsoft technology stack and utilizing Windows Communication Foundation. I was also responsible for the front-end built using Silverlight.

The game was part of the global finals of the Microsoft Imagine Cup competition in New York, 2011. << back
