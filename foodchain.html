The Moonshot
One of the UN MDGs is aiming to end hunger, achieve food security and improved nutrition and promote sustainable agriculture. The goal of this project is to create an infromation platform that:
  • allows people to track the origin of producs
  • allows companies and small farmer cooperations to get more value for their produce by showcasing the way they were produced
