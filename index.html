Social change through response-able systems that empower everyone to participate.

Thank you for finding my personal page!

I'm a Data Scientist at the Responsible AI team at Accenture as well as a contributor to the IEEE P7010 Well-being Metrics Standard for Ethical Artificial Intelligence and Autonomous Systems. It was amazing to be involved in the Assembly: Ethics and Governance of AI program in 2018, a collaboration between the Berkman Klein Center for Internet and Society at Harvard Law School and the MIT Media Lab. Previously, a research engineer at the Think Tank Team innovation lab at Samsung Research, a student and later a teaching fellow at Singularity University, also a startup co-founder in the intersection of AI, Future of Work, and Manufacturing.

Currently, doing research in the field of Fairness, Accountability and Transparency in Machine Learning, and AI For Social Good. I'm very interested in what does it mean to create social change through designing systems that empower everyone to participate through data and governance frameworks for AI, AI impact assessments, and multi-stakeholder collaboration frameworks.
